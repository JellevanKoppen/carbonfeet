# 04_target_users.md

## Overview

CarbonFeet is designed for individuals who want to understand and reduce
their personal CO₂ footprint. The product targets people who are open to
awareness, motivated by progress, and interested in improving their
lifestyle impact over time.

The app is built to be accessible to a wide audience while still
offering enough depth to feel meaningful and personal.

## Primary Target Audience

### Climate-Aware Adults (Core Segment)

**Age range:** 18--40\
**Geographic scope:** Global\
**Technical level:** Low to average

These users: - Care about sustainability - Want to live more
consciously - Are curious about their personal impact - Appreciate
simple data-driven insights

They are not necessarily experts.\
They are not necessarily activists.\
They simply want to understand and do better.

## Secondary Audience

### Climate-Curious Individuals

These users: - Have heard about climate impact - Feel they should care -
But don't know where to start

CarbonFeet serves as an entry point by: - Making CO₂ understandable -
Showing simple, visual feedback - Turning awareness into action

## Behavioral Traits of Ideal Users

Ideal users typically:

-   Use fitness trackers or budgeting apps
-   Like seeing progress over time
-   Enjoy measurable goals
-   Appreciate simple dashboards
-   Are open to adjusting habits

They respond well to: - Visual feedback - Achievements - Comparisons -
Clear progress indicators

## Motivations

Users are primarily driven by:

-   Awareness
-   Self-improvement
-   Curiosity
-   Responsibility
-   Personal values

They want to: - Understand their footprint - See how they compare to
others - Improve gradually

## Emotional Profile

Users should feel:

-   Curious when they start
-   Informed as they use the app
-   Motivated by progress
-   Proud when improving

The app should avoid making users feel: - Guilty - Judged - Overwhelmed

## Entry Personas

### Persona 1 --- The Conscious Professional

-   Age: 25--35
-   Lives in a city
-   Travels occasionally
-   Eats mixed diet
-   Already cares about sustainability

Goals: - Understand personal impact - Reduce emissions gradually - Make
smarter lifestyle choices

### Persona 2 --- The Data-Driven Improver

-   Likes metrics and tracking
-   Uses fitness or finance apps
-   Motivated by progress and numbers

Goals: - Measure lifestyle impact - Optimize behavior - Track
improvement over time

### Persona 3 --- The Curious Beginner

-   Knows climate change matters
-   Doesn't know their personal impact
-   Wants a simple way to start

Goals: - Learn - Explore - Become more aware

## Global Audience Considerations

Because CarbonFeet is designed for worldwide use:

-   Users will come from different cultures
-   Energy usage varies by country
-   Transport habits differ
-   Average emissions differ per region

The app should therefore: - Use country-based averages - Provide
contextual comparisons - Remain culturally neutral

## User Commitment Level

Expected engagement:

-   Weekly interaction
-   Adding events (flights, car usage updates, etc.)
-   Occasionally reviewing progress

Users are not expected to: - Log daily in detail - Track every single
activity

The app should stay lightweight and non-intrusive.

## Who This App Is NOT For

CarbonFeet is not primarily designed for:

-   Climate scientists
-   Policy analysts
-   Hardcore environmental activists needing precise datasets
-   Users seeking academic-level emission modeling

The focus remains on everyday individuals.

## Growth Potential Users (Future)

As the platform evolves, it may expand toward:

-   Families tracking shared impact
-   Friend groups comparing progress
-   Companies encouraging employee awareness
-   Educational environments

But the initial focus remains: Individual awareness and personal
insight.
